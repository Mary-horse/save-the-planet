How to save our planet?

Refuse plastic bags

Refuse plastic bags, because
they decompose up to 1000
years!

Don't smoke

Cigarettes pollute air 10 times
more than car exhaust.

Save water

When you don't use water,
turn it off, it will save a large
amount of water.

Recycle used materials

Take to the recycling center
unnecessary or used metal,
glass, paper and other
materials.

Discard plastic dishes

Use only reusable utensils and
try to use as many reusable
materials and objects as much
as possible.

Use energy-saving lumps

If everyone uses
energy-saving lamps and turns
off electrical appliances when
don't use it, we will save a
huge amount of energy.

Recycle batteries

One battery decomposes at
least 100 years, and one
battery contaminates with
heavy metals up to 20
meters of land.
